About Our Campus

Welcome to Navgurukul's Himachal Pradesh campus. This campus is dedicated to BCA students and follows a self-study based curriculum.Navgurukul is a non-profit organization based in India that focuses on providing quality education and skill development to underprivileged youth. It aims to empower young people, particularly from economically disadvantaged backgrounds, by offering them intensive training in software engineering and other digital skills. Here are some key points about Navgurukul: Mission and Vision: Navgurukul's mission is to create access to quality education and employment opportunities for students from marginalized communities. The vision is to bridge the gap between education and employment for underprivileged youth, enabling them to achieve financial independence and social mobility.

Our Partners

We are proud to be partnered with Eternal University and SBA to provide a high-quality education experience.

Himachal Pradesh Eternal University, located in Baru Sahib, Himachal Pradesh, has partnered with Navgurukul to offer educational opportunities to students, particularly those pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA). Navgurukul is an organization focused on providing high-quality, affordable education and skill development, especially in the tech domain. This partnership emphasizes a self-study based campus model, allowing students to learn at their own pace with the support of resources and mentorship provided by both Eternal University and Navgurukul.

Our Program

Our BCA program is designed to encourage self-study and practical learning. Students are equipped with the skills needed to excel in the tech industry.The goal of the BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications) program at Eternal University, in partnership with Navgurukul, is to provide a comprehensive and practical education in computer applications. This program aims to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the IT industry. Key objectives include: Skill Development: Emphasizing practical, hands-on learning to develop strong technical skills in programming, software development, and other IT-related areas. Self-Study and Autonomy: Promoting self-study and independent learning to prepare students for real-world challenges and continuous learning in their careers. Industry Readiness: Ensuring that the curriculum is aligned with industry requirements, making graduates job-ready with up-to-date technological skills. Holistic Education: Providing a well-rounded education that includes soft skills, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities, essential for personal and professional growth.
